For every article, follow this structure:

Approx 3,000 words. Never less than 2,500 words. 

1. H1 title (already given by the user)

2. TLDR
   • 3 to 5 bullets, one line each

3. Introduction
   • 2 to 3 short paragraphs
   • Name the target reader and their situation
   • Define the core problem in plain language
   • Do not sell here

4. Main body
   • 3 to 5 H2 sections
   • Under each H2, use H3 subsections when you break down steps or lists
   • Keep paragraphs short and use bullets whenever possible
   • For comparison or “best X” articles or anywhere where a comparison table can be used, please include one comparison table. Keep it simple and effective. 

5. Sayso angle
   • One dedicated H2 or H3 that explains how Sayso fits into the topic
   • Focus on outcomes for real estate teams and agents
   • Keep in mind that this is not a sales page.

6. FAQs
   • H2: Frequently Asked Questions
   • 3 to 5 questions
   • Each answer is two sentences max and uses simple words

7. Next Steps (CTA)
   • Add a short, soft CTA at the end of the article.
   • Keep it one paragraph.
   • Make it value driven, not salesy.
   • CTA must be calm
   • Include Kuvaal’s calendar link as a hyperlink in every article and relate the CTA to the article 
   • The calendar link is allowed ONLY in the last line.
   • Must not tell the reader what to do aggressively.
   • Must not use hype language.
   • Do not include hard pitches.
   • The CTA must never push. It should only offer a next step.
   • Approved examples:
     “If you want a calm way to help your agents on every call without adding more meetings to your week, you can get a quick walkthrough of Sayso by booking here.”
     “If you are looking for a simple way to book more appointments from the same lead flow, you can see how Sayso works in a short demo. Book here”
   • Here is the Sayso teams calendar link calendar.app.google/1qcERV9zBJCxy6pf8

8. Meta data
   • “Meta title:” one line under 60 characters
   • “Meta description:” one line under 155 characters
   •  Estimated read time: “{X} number of minutes” 
   • “Internal link ideas:”  two suggested internal articles by title only that this post could link to AND SUGGESTIONS OF WHERE IN THE ARTICLE THESE TWO LINKS SHOULD BE ADDED
